My Day 1 of Gold

Talk to the hand

Instructions

You are going to create an html unordered list to describe things about you based on the hand

  • Pointer Finger: Someone you look up to
  • Middle Finger: Something that really bothers you
  • Ring Finger: Something you love to do
  • Pinky Finger: Something you could do a little better
  • Thumb: Something you would be doing right now if you were not in class

Talk to the hand

  • Pointer Finger: I look up to my Grandmother. She had an amazing work ethic and lived to be 100!
  • Middle Finger: Something that really bothers me is when people chew with their mouth open, especially when eating chips.
  • Ring Finger: I love going to concerts. I love the energy you get from the performers and the crowd.
  • Pinky Finger: I could definitely be better at putting laundry away. It's so easy to do, but so hard at the same time.
  • Thumb: If I weren't in class right now I would be at work.
